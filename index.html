About the Club

Founded in 1993 by students of the then Faculty of Letters, Department of English Language Teaching, the language club is a student organization that currently continues its activities under the Hasan Ali Yücel Faculty of Education, and draws its human resources from Istanbul University students who have strong social skills, self-confidence, and the ability to create added value for themselves and their field through sharing.
